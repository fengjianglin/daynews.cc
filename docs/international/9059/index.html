盤點全球26個國家的爽心美食（上）

2019-12-06 2,927條評論 4,363次閱讀 201人點贊

食物不只讓我們保持身體健康，還讓我們心情愉悅。英國的炸魚薯條、美國的烤芝士三明治、希臘的慕薩卡、法國的洋蔥湯……每個國家都有讓人舒適的美味食物。來看看世界各國值得一嘗的爽心美食。

Photo by sheri silver on Unsplash

FRANCE: French onion soup

法國：法式洋蔥湯

French onion soup is winter comfort food as it should be: rich, warm, and blanketed under a generous portion of creamy cheese. It’s best enjoyed with a slice of crusty bread.法式洋蔥湯是冬季令人感到舒適的食物：口感濃郁的熱湯上面是厚厚一層奶油芝士。最好配上一片脆皮麵包享用。

Photo by Artur Rutkowski on Unsplash

JAPAN: Ramen

日本：日本拉麵

From the sodium-heavy instant packs found in every American college dorm to the Michelin-star winning joint in Tokyo, Japan, ramen has developed into a global obsession.從美國大學宿舍里隨處可見的高鈉速食麵，到日本東京的米其林餐廳，拉麵讓全世界為之痴迷。

內幕網截圖

CANADA: Poutine

加拿大：普丁

We have Montreal to thank for Canada’s unofficial national dish, poutine (which also sometimes goes by its informal nickname, “Canadian salad”). The iconic dish consists of crispy, golden fries topped with cheese curds and drenched in gravy.加拿大民間國菜「普丁」源自蒙特利爾，有時也用非正式的綽號「加拿大沙拉」來稱呼。這道經典美食是淋著乳酪凝乳和肉汁的脆脆的金黃薯條。

curd[kɜːrd]: n. 凝乳；凝乳狀物

內幕網截圖

MEXICO: Chilaquiles

墨西哥：汁拉貴司

Chilaquiles — which translates to “in a sauce of chiles” from Nahuatl — is a traditional Mexican dish consisting of heaps of crisp tortilla triangles tossed in a pan with salsa, and then piled sky-high with crumbled cheese and crema. Even better, chilaquiles are often enjoyed for breakfast.汁拉貴司——納瓦特爾語譯為「辣椒醬」——是墨西哥的一道傳統菜肴。把成堆的酥脆的三角形玉米餅放入平底鍋中，撒上洋蔥番茄辣醬，然後用碎乳酪和油脂堆得高高的。早餐時吃這道菜最棒了。

tortilla[tɔːrˈtiːə]: n. 玉米粉薄烙餅，玉米粉圓餅

內幕網截圖

GREAT BRITAIN: Fish and chips

英國：炸魚薯條

If you’re harboring any reservations about just how delicious battered fish and crispy fries taste when dipped in a creamy tartar sauce, do yourself a favor and embark on a mission to try these top fish and chip shops in every region of the UK.如果你對蘸著奶油塔塔醬的脆炸魚薯條的美味程度有任何保留意見，勞煩到英國各地的頂級炸魚薯條店去嘗一嘗吧。

Photo by Valentin B. Kremer on Unsplash

AUSTRIA: Wiener schnitzel

奧地利：維也納炸肉排

Wiener schnitzel, the national dish of Austria, consists of boneless, mallet-thinned meat (usually veal) that is then breaded and deep-fried until it reaches prime crispiness.奧地利國菜維也納炸肉排採用剔去骨頭的、用木槌打薄的肉（通常是小牛肉），裹上麵包屑，油炸至酥脆。

Photo by Nerfee Mirandilla on Unsplash

ITALY: Spaghetti alla carbonara

義大利：奶油蘑菇義大利面

Originally from the region of Lazio (which is around Rome), this decadent spaghetti is a creamy mix of crispy bacon, velvety olive oil, fatty egg yolk, and nutty Parmesan cheese.起源於拉齊奧地區（位於羅馬附近）的頂級義大利面是由酥脆的培根、可口的橄欖油、流油的蛋黃和散發著堅果香氣的帕馬森乾酪做成的，擁有奶油般的柔滑口感。

內幕網截圖

AUSTRALIA: Sausage rolls

澳大利亞：熱狗卷

Think of these Aussie favorites as the larger, heartier, and more sophisticated version of pigs-in-a-blanket.請把澳大利亞人最愛的這種食物想像成更大、更豐盛、做法更複雜的熱狗卷。

內幕網截圖

POLAND: Pierogi

波蘭：波蘭餃子

Pierogies are boiled dumplings that are typically filled with a mixture of potatoes and cheese curds, and often enjoyed with a generous dollop of sour cream and caramelized onions.波蘭餃子是以土豆和乾酪凝塊為餡的餃子，煮熟後通常搭配著一大團酸奶油和焦糖洋蔥食用。

dollop [ˈdɑːləp]: n. 團，塊

內幕網截圖

ISRAEL: Matzo ball soup

以色列：猶太麵包球湯

Matzo balls — which are made from matzo meal and eggs, and often consumed during Passover — are doughy, rich soup dumplings that have a delicious tendency to soak up all the flavors of the broth in which they’re submerged.麵包球由未發酵的薄餅粉和雞蛋製成，通常在逾越節期間食用。它是一種湯糰，口感鬆軟，湯汁豐富，吸收了肉湯里的各種味道。

CHINA: Congee

中國：粥

Congee — a soothing type of rice porridge that is so extensively cooked that the individual grains of rice coalesce into an almost homogeneous base — is popular in many East Asian countries, and is often fortified with different garnishes and ingredients to amplify the flavor.中國的米粥是一種撫慰人心的食物，在許多東亞國家都很受歡迎。米粥經過長時間熬煮，米粒都成了軟爛的米糊，通常會添加不同的配菜和佐料來增添風味。

homogeneous[ˌhoʊməˈdʒiːniəs]: adj. 同質的

garnish[ˈɡɑːrnɪʃ]: n. （為增加色香味而添加的）配菜，裝飾品

Photo by Pixzolo Photography on Unsplash

AMERICA: Grilled cheese

美國：烤芝士三明治

The simple comfort mainstay known as the grilled cheese sandwich can come dressed up or au naturel — just as long as it comes with a side of tomato soup for dunking.烤芝士三明治是一道簡單又常見的爽心美食，既可以盛裝上桌，也可以直接吃，只要配上一碗西紅柿湯，浸泡著吃就很美味。

內幕網截圖

GERMANY: Bratwurst

德國：德式小香腸

Arguably Germany’s most widely valued culinary contribution, bratwursts are thick German-style sausages that are frequently paired with stewed sauerkraut and a tall, cool beer.德國最受推崇的美食可能要算德式小香腸了，它是一種口味濃重的德國風味香腸，經常與燴酸菜和冰涼的大杯啤酒搭配在一起食用。

sauerkraut[ˈsaʊərkraʊt]: n. 一種德國泡菜
