春寒賜浴華清池，溫泉水滑洗凝脂。廣東的“華清池”又在哪裡？

2019-12-13 2,354條評論 3,302次閱讀 1784人點贊

中國醫療礦泉專家陳炎冰在《礦泉與療養》一書中認為，溫泉一般含有多種活性作用的微量元素，有一定的礦化度，泉水溫度常高於30℃以上。溫礦泉可對以下疾病具有醫療作用：肥胖症、運動系統疾病（如創傷、慢性風濕性關節炎等）、神經系統疾病（神經損傷、神經炎等），早期輕度心血管系統疾病、痛風、皮膚病等。

廣東大部份溫泉水屬於低溫泉水，所含礦物質豐富，同時，善於養生的廣東人，更在溫泉中玩了不少的花樣，加了諸如紅酒、中藥材、花草植物等各種物質，使得溫泉的醫療和美容效果更佳。在廣東，泡溫泉泡的是養生。

With cold waves continuously advancing southward, Guangdong is now stepping into winter. On cold winter days, there is nothing better than immersing yourself in hot springs, to relax and heal both body and mind.

With dozens of hot springs in the market, how can we tell the good ones from the bad? What are real hot springs like? Silk Road Post will offer a guide for you to find the best hot springs in Guangdong.

What are real hot springs like?

According to national standards, hot springs should have a temperature higher than 34℃ and contain chemical constituents beneficial to human bodies, such as fluorine, lithium and radon. Their PH (potential for hydrogen) value and mineralization are also important criteria.

Real hot springs smell a little like rotten eggs. Normally, if the temperature of the spring water would reach 50-60℃, a yellow layer of calcium fluoride can be seen building up at corners or edges of the pool.

Guangdong Hot Spring Hotel 廣東溫泉賓館

The Guangdong Hot Spring Hotel recently won the 4th Golden Hot-Spring Award. Covering an area of over 270,000 square meters, this garden-style hotel boasts comprehensive facilities, refreshing natural scenery and first-class services. Dozens of hot spring pools of different functions are equipped to satisfy customers’ needs. It would be an ideal choice for women to bath in those alkaline soda hot springs, as they supposedly help moisturize and beautify the skin and have special therapeutic effects for skin and motor-system ailments.


Address: No. 112 Wenquan Donglu, Wenquan town, Conghua district 從化溫泉鎮溫泉東路112號

碧水灣溫泉度假村位於廣州從化流溪河畔，地處「從化80公里綠色旅遊走廊」的中心，四周層巒疊嶂，樹木蔥鬱，碧水環繞。總佔地240畝，建築面積4萬多平方米，是一家由民航中南空管局投資、按五星級標準建造的集餐飲、住宿、娛樂及大型露天溫泉為一體的溫泉主題度假村。

碧水灣溫泉屬低氡珍稀蘇打溫泉，富含二十多種有益人體微量元素，如鈣、鐵、鋅、鉀、硒、鈉、鎂、銨、氟、錳、鈷、鉬、釩、錫、砷、鉻、硅、碘、銅等，其中硅、氡、碘、砷、為含有醫療效果的特殊成份;鋅、釩、硅、砷、氟、碘為人體所必須的微量元素。

Bishuiwan Hotspring Resort 碧水灣溫泉度假村

Surrounded by the Liuxi River and lush trees, the Bishuiwan Hotspring Resort is known for its picturesque scenery and attentive services,with more than 30 outdoor hot springs pools boasting both recreational and therapeutic functions. The radon springs there are effective in boosting metabolism, relieving fatigue and improving sleep. Recreational facilities – such as swimming pools and football pitches – are also offered to better serve customers.

Add: No.353 Yuquan Dadao, Conghua district 從化區御泉大道353號

Zhuhai Ocean Spring Resort 珠海海泉灣度假區

Situated on the west coast of Zhuhai, the Zhuhai Ocean Spring Resort is honored as the first national-level demonstration area of tourism and leisure in China. The rare ocean hot springs gushing from the depth of the sea contain over 30 minerals and trace elements beneficial to human bodies, like sodium, calcium and magnesium. Characterized by landscapes and buildings with unique Moroccan and Mediterranean styles, hot spring pools in different shapes can offer visitors diverse and amazing bathing experiences. Beyond hot springs, several themed parks – Pirates of the Caribbean, the Mermaid, Mysterious Island and others – are open for tourists to explore.

Add: Wenquan Lu, Pingsha town, Doumen district, Zhuhai city 珠海市斗門區平沙鎮溫泉路

Gudou Hot Spring Valley 江門古兜溫泉

Located at the southern foot of Gudou Mountain, the Gudou Hot Spring Valley boasts a combination of sea water hot springs and radon hot springs, the only in Guangdong province, making it a good performer in both rejuvenating tired bodies and treating rheumatic diseases. Distinctive hot springs areas, featuring architectural structures in Japanese and European styles, are favored by many tourists for they render a “time-traversing” experience. Lodgings in both eastern and western styles also promise visitors an unforgettable stay here.

Add: Gudou Hot Spring Resort, Yanan town, Xinhui district, Jiangmen city 江門新會區崖南古兜度假村
